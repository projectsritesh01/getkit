The defining trait is **structural alignment**.

A digital product kit:

A template marketplace gives you options.

# Why Most Digital Products Stall Before Launch 

Let’s break this down systematically.

## 1. Decision Fatigue 

When founders face too many options, cognitive overload slows action. Behavioral research consistently shows excessive choice reduces follow-through.

## 2. Tool Fragmentation 

Switching between:

- Canva

- Email platforms

- Funnel builders

- Automation tools

Without a defined workflow increases setup time.

## 3. Asset Mismatch 

Templates purchased from different sources often conflict in:

- Typography

- Tone

- Layout logic

- Funnel sequencing

Each mismatch requires manual correction.

Correction consumes time.

Time delays monetization.
